Safe Landing give guest lecture at the University of Sheffield

20 Dec, 2024

 
 

✈️ It was a pleasure to give a guest lecture on the challenges and opportunities of “Sustainable Aviation” to aerospace and engineering students at The University of Sheffield.

🙋🙋‍♂️ There was a great discussion with a room full of ambitious young engineers motivated to take on these challenges in their future careers!

🎅 It was a great way to finish-off the year!

🙏 Thanks to Aerospace Engineering Society – University of Sheffield and ‘Engineers Without Borders’ Sheffield for the invite.

🎓 We regularly give talks to groups of college or university students and receive requests from lecturers looking to incorporate guest presentations on sustainability into their courses. Or alternatively, as today, we receive requests directly from students.

💡 We’re keen to foster debate and talk to anybody interested in our perspectives and positions – so please get in touch if you’d like to arrange this with us in 2025!
