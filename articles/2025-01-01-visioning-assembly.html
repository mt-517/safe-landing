Visioning Assembly

We warmly invite all aviation workers and enthusiasts to help shape a bold and sustainable future for our industry at our upcoming Visioning Assembly.

Our goal is to create a transformational vision for the future of air travel that is distinct from the current flightpath in a creative, fun and engaging way.

Sign up here to take part

 

Aviation has transformed our world and has long served humanity by connecting cultures, peoples and families. It has powered global trade, pioneered huge advances in technology, and inspired generations with the wonder of flight.

It supports over 88 million jobs worldwide, and continues to drive innovation in technology, safety, and efficiency. Yet today, the industry stands at a pivotal moment where we must acknowledge the pressing challenges we face.

The impact of aviation on our climate is significant, as the industry accounts for approximately 2% of carbon emissions, with its global heating impact around 2-3 times that. While the aviation sector aspires to achieve Net Zero by 2050, it is also projected to double in size by 2040. This trajectory raises critical questions about our industry's sustainability and our responsibility to align with Earth's delicate systems.

 

In this Visioning Assembly we aim to explore and develop alternative pathways that empower the aviation industry to thrive, while safeguarding the environment. We believe in a future where millions of job opportunities for aviation workers continue to be created and the beauty of flying can be enjoyed for generations to come.

We are excited to announce our second Visioning Assembly: “Creating the Future of Aviation.”

With the UK’s rich history as a pioneer of the jet age, we believe it is uniquely positioned to lead the charge toward a greener future. Your insights and contributions are essential as we work together to craft an inspiring vision of what aviation can become.

Join us in London, where expert facilitators from Safe Landing will guide a select group of aviation workers and enthusiasts in collaboratively shaping a positive and sustainable future for the industry we all love.

The outcomes of these discussions will culminate in a comprehensive report that will be shared with industry leaders, policymakers, and the media, ensuring that our collective voice is heard. This report will also be accessible on our website for all aviation workers to reference.

We hope our approach to collaborative democracy will inspire not only the aviation sector but also other high-carbon industries to embrace similar constructive assemblies. Together, we can build a robust platform for innovation and cooperation, where diverse voices contribute to shaping the future of our industries.

This is just the beginning, and we need your passion and input to soar into a brighter future for aviation.
