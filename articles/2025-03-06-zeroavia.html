Safe Landing visit ZeroAvia

2025-3-6 • Interviews

On Thursday 6th March, members of the Safe Landing community (Aayaan, Andy, Diana, Finlay, George, James and Todd) visited the ZeroAvia hangars at Cotswold Airport, UK – for a tour round their offices, hangars and testing facilities.

It was a great opportunity to hang-out while learning about the operations of one of the most innovative aerospace companies operating in the UK.

ZeroAvia are one of the few companies globally developing hydrogen-electric powertrains for commercial aviation.

We were shown round by Dominic Weeks, Head of External Affairs and Marketing, at ZeroAvia. Dominic leads their marketing, communications and policy-focused work globally – seeking to ensure awareness, interest and adoption of the company’s technology. We discussed:

  • What motivated Dominic’s interest in aviation and sustainability
  • What ZeroAvia are working on today
  • ZeroAvia’s vision for the sustainable future of air travel
  • Advice to aviation workers and students interested in the sustainability of our industry
