The next few years are critical for aviation, and for Safe Landing — and we need you.
We’re inviting all community members, industry stakeholders, and anyone passionate about a sustainable future for aviation to an open session to decide together what our top priorities should be as we move through 2025 and beyond.

We want to hear what matters most to you, and how you believe Safe Landing can make the biggest impact.

This is more than just a meeting — it’s a chance to set the direction of our organisation for the years ahead, influence real change, and ensure that workers and the planet stay at the heart of aviation’s transition.

Your ideas, your experiences, and your passion will help drive the action we take next. Below are two actions for you to take if you can – sign up for the Zoom strategy session and take our very quick poll (it’s just one question!).

Date: 28/05/2025
Location: Virtual (Zoom)
Time: 12:00pm British Summer Time

Be part of shaping our vision for the future — a world where aviation flourishes in harmony with nature, and the beauty of flying can be enjoyed by all, for generations to come.
